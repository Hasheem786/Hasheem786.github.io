Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data stored online?
No. Your ledger data is stored locally on your device unless you choose to export it.

Do I need an internet connection?
No. The app works offline for normal day-to-day usage.

How can I report a bug?
Please email us with details about the issue and, if possible, include screenshots.
